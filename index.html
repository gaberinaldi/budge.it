TIME TO LEARN HOW TO EARN!

Our budget tracking website, your personal finance companion, is on a mission to empower you with the tools and insights you need to take control of your financial future. We understand that managing your money can be complex, and that's why we've designed our platform to be straightforward and user-friendly. Below we will help you learn where you should be saving so you can reach your financial goals!

Rent/Mortgage

Rent (30-35%): Rent is a significant portion of many people's monthly expenses. The percentage can vary widely depending on the location, housing costs, and the size of the living space.

Auto

Auto (15-20%): Auto expenses, including insurance and maintenance, can range from 15% to 20% of a household's budget. This percentage can fluctuate with the cost of vehicle maintenance, and insurance.

Utilities

Utilities (5-10%): Utilities, including electricity, water, and heating, typically account for about 5-10% of a household's expenses. This can vary based on the region and the size of the household.

Groceries

Groceries (10-20%): Grocery expenses can range from 10% to 20% of a household's budget. This can vary significantly based on dietary habits, the number of people in the household, and the cost of living in the area.

Gas

Gas (5-10%): Gasoline expenses can account for about 5-10% of a household's budget, depending on the number of vehicles in the household and fuel consumption habits.

Savings

Savings (15-20%): Aiming for 15-20% of income to be saved is a common financial goal. This can help cover emergencies, pay for unexpected expenses, and build a financial cushion.
